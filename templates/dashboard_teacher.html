Teacher's Guide

Use the Mark Attendance feature to record student attendance for your subject. Here's how:

  1. Click on the "Mark Today's Attendance" button
  2. Select the date (defaults to today)
  3. Check the boxes for students who are present
  4. Click "Save Attendance" to record the data
Mark Attendance Now

The Attendance Reports section allows you to:

  • View attendance percentage for each student
  • Monitor which students are frequently absent
  • Generate attendance summaries
View Reports

For effective attendance management:

  • Mark attendance at the beginning of each class
  • Regularly check attendance reports to identify patterns
  • Contact students with attendance below 75%
  • Update attendance records promptly if there are any corrections
